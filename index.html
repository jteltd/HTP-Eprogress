ILR Compliant MI System

Our product EProgress is a cloud based management system for Training Providers and Employers with ESFA contracts.

As a Software Provider we have to comply with the Education and Skills Funding Agency (ESFA) ILR technical specification. This specification changes annually and therefore so does our software.

Training Management Software

As part of the Government's ongoing commitment to Apprenticeships within England the Apprenticeship Levy was introduced in May 2017, leading to a more employer led delivery.

To be able to fulfil these contracts, Employers and Training Providers who are delivering Work Based Learning Apprenticeships and Traineeships, will need to be able to manage the learner journey with training management software which has the following functionality:-

  • ESFA ILR compliant software
  • An intuitive secure cloud based system
  • Learner tracking and progression
  • Framework and qualification maintenance
  • Produce ESFA XML export files
  • Learner reviews
  • Destination and Progression reporting
  • OFSTED success reports

If you are considering using an ESFA ILR learner entry tool and are concerned with prohibitive setup costs of Training Management Software you will be happy to hear that EProgress is a very cost-effective proposition.

As a small and agile organisation we focus on delivering a service as well as delivering software. So when you have a question you can call and speak to a person who can help. All of the team have extensive experience in this field.
